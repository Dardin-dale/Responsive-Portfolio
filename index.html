About Me

I am currently working as a Quality Control Analyst developing pH monitoring devices for in vitro fertilization at BCSI. My current projects include maintaining our linux based software and adding automation tools to our python based manufacturing tools.

I am origianlly from the quiet mountian town of Bend, Oregon. I moved to Seattle in 2011 to pursue my bachelor's in chemistry from the University of Washington.

Outside of work my hobbies include: Swing Dancing (Lindy, Balboa, Shag), playing the Saxophone, and rowing. I am also an active member of the North Seattle Homebrewing Club which won the NHBC Gambrius award in 2018.
